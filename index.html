Who We Are

About our textile house

Prime Fabric is a trusted textile manufacturing and stitching company specializing in bulk production solutions for institutions, brands, NGOs, healthcare, and public programs.

As a subsidiary brand of Pak Rose Enterprises, we deliver uniforms, hospital textiles, bedding products, relief supplies, promotional items, and custom fabric-based solutions with disciplined processes and reliable output.
